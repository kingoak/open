A Gathering on Trans Inclusion in Open Source

June 20th, 2017 - 10am-5pm PDT - San Francisco

Learn More Apply to Attend



Why OpenHouse Summit?

Trans and gender non-conforming people have relied on open source technologies to support the community around us. OpenHouse Summit is an opportunity to continue building and advocating for inclusive uses of open source that serves diverse populations.

Who Should Attend?

This one day summit is for trans, gender non-conforming, agender, non-binary founders & technologists creating in the tech community to share, collaborate and learn across a varity of topics related to open source. OpenHouse Summit is free to participate and lodging/travel to San Francisco will be covered if invited to attend.

Apply to Participate

Are you a trans founder using open source technologies? A rising leader in tech? Someone who wants to share space with other trans technologists who are building in and for the community?

Apply to participate as a summit attendee and/or to give a lighting talk on a topic of your choice related to the theme of open source & trans inclusion.
